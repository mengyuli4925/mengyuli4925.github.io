N.Y.C DUELING PLAN CONCERT

I designed this poster for a piano concert. When I think of the piano, the first thing comes into my mind is black and white keys, so, I only use black and white color to design my poster. Black and white are oppo- site but unified, they give us a strong visual impact. In order to make the work looks more comprehensive and harmonious. I used a lot of regular composition formats. In order to make the work full of spatial layering, I used different expressions to make the layer rich by using the dense arrangement of points or lines. Also, the black and white color has a transition by changing the black block surface to get the level change.
